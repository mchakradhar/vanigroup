Compliances

WHOGMP (CDSCO)

Vani Pharma Labs Limited is following the cGMP ICH Q7A Guidelines and WHOGMP Guidelines, test of the Expert Committee on Specification for Pharmaceutical preparations 32nd Report (WHO technical report series 823). The Unit got certified for WHOGMP on 4 th September 2019 by the Director, Drugs Control Administration, Andhra Pradesh and Inspected by Central Drug Authorities

ISO 14001:2015

Vani Pharma has obtained UL Registered Firm Mark by achieving ISO 14001:2015 Registration from Underwriters Laboratories Inc. ® in accordance with the Environmental Management System Requirements of ISO 14001:2015 on August 30, 2018 under Registration No. 20003462UM.

ISO 9001:2015

Vani Pharma has obtained UL Registered Firm Mark by achieving ISO 9001:2015 Registration from Underwriters Laboratories Inc. ® in accordance with the requirements of ENISO9001:2015, BSENISO9001:2015, ANSI/ASQ Q9001:2000 on August 30, 2018 under Registration No. 20003462QM15.
